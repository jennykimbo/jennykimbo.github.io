KIRO Radio does a giveaway every weekday morning for two tickets to the next home game for the Seattle Seahawks. Listen to 97.3FM at 7:30am to hear the ticket giveaway question. There will be a cue to call later on in the day when you need to be the twelfth caller AND have the correct answer to the question in order to win the tickets!

Second Ticket gives away either up to $500 worth of tickets, a 20% discount, or up to $10,000 in cash! All you have to do is go to their website and make a prediction about the score of the next game. Depending on how close you are to the actual score, determines what you win!

Shell is doing a promotion this year connected to the Shell Fuel Rewards program. All you need to do is sign up for a Shell Rewards Membership (if you don't already have one), and everytime you buy at least 8 gallons of gas you're entered for a chance to win a pair of tickets to a regular season game as well as a pair of pregame sideline passes for a regular season game!
